Eggless Chocolate Cake

  • Description

    • Eggless chocolate cake is a rich, moist, and indulgent dessert that proves you don’t need eggs to achieve perfect texture and flavor. Made with simple pantry ingredients like cocoa powder, flour, milk, and butter or oil, this cake is soft, tender, and deeply chocolatey. Ideal for birthdays, celebrations, or everyday cravings, it pairs beautifully with chocolate ganache, frosting, or a dusting of powdered sugar. Loved by vegetarians and chocolate lovers alike, it’s a reliable, crowd-pleasing treat.

  • Ingrediants

  • 4 ½ cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons vanilla extract

  • Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Add sugar and mix until well combined.
  3. Pour in water, oil, and vanilla; mix thoroughly until batter is smooth. Pour batter into the prepared baking dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour.
  5. Garnish it with some cream. Enjoy the desert
